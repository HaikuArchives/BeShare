Compiling BeShare
======================

Really quick compilation instructions:
1. run ./getMUSCLE.sh to fetch a copy of MUSCLE
 - OR -
1. download a copy of MUSCLE: https://public.msli.com/lcs/muscle/ & extract into a folder called "muscle" here in the root

2. change your GCC to GCC4 ("setarch x86" on GCC2hybrid builds)
3. "make" in src
